The Best Movies According to Anamarija

My top 3 Movies of all time.

Split

This is an excellently acted, directed and thought out movie. It's intense, funny at some parts, and it is incredibly well thought out.

Schindler's List

Great and powerfull movie, based on true story of a heroic Oskar Schindler, who saved thousands of Jewish lives during the Holocaust

Spirited Away

It's a work of magic It is an unforgettable, thrilling and enchanting anime movie. Easily one of the best movies I've ever watched and the best movie in Studio Ghibli
